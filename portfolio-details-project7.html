Machine Learning for Smarter Wastewater Management

I am the project manager behind Bergen kommune's data-driven optimization of the wastewater infrastructure.

We gather real-time data from millions of sensors using Azure Data Factory, primarily focused on wastewater levels across over 260 pumping stations.

Our Azure Databricks notebooks handle ETL, feature engineering, and model training. The outputs are automatically consumed by interactive dashboards in Microsoft Power BI, giving operators and decision-makers deep insights into system performance, overflow events, and predictive flow trends.

I’ve led the full solution from data acquisition to visualization — including infrastructure design, pipeline scheduling, and user-friendly dashboards for municipal teams.

This initiative reduces overflow, improves maintenance planning, and helps Bergen achieve smarter, more sustainable urban water management through machine learning.
