About the Game

“Pi to the Moon” is a funny and chaotic 2D platformer where a Pi coin is propelled by gas to reach the moon. Collect coins, avoid falling through gaps, and unlock power-ups — all rendered in custom cartoon-style graphics.

The game features AdMob integration for monetization using rewarded video ads. When you die, you can choose to watch an ad and revive instantly — a player-friendly monetization method.

It’s built with Phaser.js, published with Netlify, and soon deployable as a mobile app using Capacitor + AdMob rewarded ads.
