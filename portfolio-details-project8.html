About the game

“Pi to the Moon” is a chaotic 2D platformer where you play as a Pi coin — powered by flatulence 💨. The goal? Make it to the moon. The method? Gas propulsion.

You’ll jump, hover, and blast your way across moving platforms, dodging gaps and grabbing floating coins. Miss a jump? No worries — watch an ad to revive and try again! 🚀

Game features:

  • 💥 Long-press to boost your jumps with powerful bursts
  • 🪙 Collect coins (yes, like real Pi coins!)
  • 💔 Die by falling into holes — then get a second chance by watching an ad
  • 🎮 Simple controls, addictive gameplay, and lots of fart sounds
  • 📱 Built for mobile and ready to become an APK with rewarded ads via AdMob

Built with Phaser.js, published on Netlify, and soon blasting off to mobile via Capacitor + AdMob.
